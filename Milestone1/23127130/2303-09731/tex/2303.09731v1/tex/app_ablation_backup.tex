Similar to the results shown in Section \ref{sec:Experiment:results:ablation}, the effectiveness of our proposed defense on PointPillars and PV-RCNN is also insensitive to different choices of the model structure of LOP, while the value of $B$ will still make influence on the precision of the 3D object detector and the defense effectiveness of LOP under attacks. In short, the LOP with a higher $B$ also performs better on PointPillars and PV-RCNN.
