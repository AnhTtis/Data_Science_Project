\textbf{Algorithm 1}: Overlapped Speech Simulation
\vspace{2pt}
\hline
\small
$ $ 1: given a batch of speech utterances $U = {u^i}^{B}_{i=1}$ with batch size $B$, mixing probability $p$, a set of DNS noises \cite{Reddy2021} $N = {n^i}^{M}_{i=1}$, with size $M$,   

\hspace{10pt} mixing noise probability $p_n$, max-num-speakers $ms$, the min/max location to mix relative to primary start $O_{min}$, $O_{max}$, the min/max 

\hspace{10pt} length ratio of primary length $L_{min}$, $L_{max}$ for determining mix length

$ $ 2: choose $S$ utterances $U^S \subset U$ by Bernoulli sampling with probability $p$ 

$ $ 3: choose $n$ number of speakers to mix from discrete uniform distribution with probability $P(n = x)=\frac{1}{ms - 1}, x \in \{2, ..., ms\}$

$ $ 4: \textbf{for} each primary utterance ${\textnormal u}^{pri} \in U^S$ \textbf{do}

$ $ 5: \hspace{5pt} calculate the energy of the primary utterance $E^{pri} \xleftarrow{} \frac{\sum{{\textnormal u}^{pri} . {\textnormal u}^{pri}}}{len({\textnormal u}^{pri})}$

$ $ 6: \hspace{5pt} let $X$ be the list of utterances to mix with

$ $ 7: \hspace{5pt} \textbf{for} $n$ number of speakers \textbf{do}

$ $ 8: \hspace{15pt} sample a random value $v$ from the continuous uniform distribution ${\cal U}(0, 1)$

$ $ 9: \hspace{15pt} \textbf{if} $v > p_n$ \textbf{then} sample an utterance ${\textnormal u}^{sec}$ from discrete uniform distribution with probability $P({\textnormal u}^{sec} = x) = \frac{1}{B}, x \in U, x \notin X$

$ $ 10: \hspace{10pt} \textbf{else} sample a noise ${\textnormal u}^{sec}$ from discrete uniform distribution with probability $P({\textnormal u}^{sec} = x) = \frac{1}{M}, x \in N$

%$ $ 13: \hspace{6pt} \textbf{end if}

$ $ 11: \hspace{10pt} add ${\textnormal u}^{sec}$ to $X$

$ $ 12: \hspace{10pt} sample the mixing energy ratio $r$ from the continuous uniform distribution ${\cal U}(-5, 5)$

$ $ 13: \hspace{10pt} sample the mix length $l$ from discrete uniform ... \maryam{update}

$ $ 14: \hspace{10pt} sample a start position $s^{pri}$ from discrete uniform ... \maryam{update}

$ $ 15: \hspace{10pt} calculate the energy of the secondary utterance $E^{sec} \xleftarrow{} \frac{\sum{{\textnormal u}^{sec} . {\textnormal u}^{sec}}}{len({\textnormal u}^{sec})}$

$ $ 16: \hspace{10pt} calculate the mixing scale $scl \xleftarrow{} \sqrt{\frac{E^{pri}}{10^{\frac{r}{10}}E^{sec}}}$

$ $ 17: \hspace{10pt} ${\textnormal u}^{pri}[s^{pri}: s^{pri} + l] \xleftarrow{} {\textnormal u}^{pri}[s^{pri}: s^{pri} + l] + scl \times {\textnormal u}^{sec}[0 : l]$

%$ $ 19: \hspace{2pt} \textbf{end for}

%$ $ 20: \textbf{end for}

$ $ 18: \textbf{return $U$}
