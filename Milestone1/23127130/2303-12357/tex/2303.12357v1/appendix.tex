\section*{Appendix}

This is the appendix for the submission: \emph{Wasserstein Adversarial Examples on Univariant Time Series Data}, including  the full version of the experimental results.

\subsection*{Attack Success Rate (ASR)}

In this section, we present the full version of the experimental results on the benchmark time series classification datasets from the publicly available UCR archive \cite{UCRArchive}. The datasets are selected under the ``ECG" category for ECG based diagnosis tasks, where an adversarial attack is a potential security concern, including ECG200, ECG5000, ECGFiveDays, NonInvasiveFatalECG\_Thorax1, and NonInvasiveFatalECG\_Thorax2. 

We adopt and evaluate the target deep learning models from \cite{ismail2019deep} including: Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP) \cite{gardner1998artificial}, Fully Connected Networks (FCN)\cite{long2015fully}, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) \cite{krizhevsky2012imagenet} and Residual Networks (ResNet) \cite{he2016deep}.

ECG5000 is the Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center (BIDMC) congestive heart failure database, consisting of records of 15 subjects, with severe congestive heart failure.
