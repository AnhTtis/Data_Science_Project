\section{Ethics Statement}
The human Chinese character naming experiment has received IRB approval.
% from Hunter College, City University of New York (Project 2021-2160-Hunter). 
The participants were recruited online and in person. The selection criteria include: 1) native speaker of Mandarin; 2) able to read and write in traditional Chinese scripts; 3) able to read and write in pinyin. The participants were asked to complete an online questionnaire on qualtrics on their phone or computer. The electronic informed consent was obtained from the participants at the beginning of the experiment. There were four screening questions to make sure that the participants fit the selection criterion. All the participants passed the screening. The only personally information collected are age and education level. The participants have the options to not answer these two questions. No other personal information (e.g. name, email address, occupation) were collected. All the data were anonymized with randomly generated letter string as the identifier. The participants were not paid for completing this experiment.
