\appendix

Define 
\[\e_\epsilon=\frac{\tilde{e}(T;S_{\mu},\epsilon) - \tilde{e}(T;S_{\mu},0)}{\epsilon}.\]
In this appendix to Theorem 1, we prove that 
\begin{equation}\label{a:switch}
\mathbb{E}\left(\underset{\epsilon \downarrow 0}{\mathrm{l.i.m.}}~\e_\epsilon\right)=
\lim_{\epsilon \downarrow 0}\mathbb{E}(\e_\epsilon),
\end{equation}
under the assumption that the {\it limit in the mean} of $\{\e_{\epsilon}\}_{\epsilon >0}$,
denoted as $\underset{\epsilon \downarrow 0}{\mathrm{l.i.m.}}~\e_\epsilon$, exists; that is, 
for a sequence $\{\epsilon_n\}_{n=1}^\infty$ decreasing to $0$, we have 
\[ \lim_{m,n \to \infty}\mathbb{E}\left(|\e_{\epsilon_n}-\e_{\epsilon_m}|^2\right)=0.\]
Since Eq.~\eqref{a:switch} involves two limits, we rewrite it as
\[\mathbb{E}\left(\underset{~\epsilon' \downarrow 0}{\mathrm{l.i.m.}}~\e_{\epsilon'}\right)=
\lim_{\epsilon \downarrow 0}\mathbb{E}(\e_\epsilon)\]
Using the definition of the right-hand side limit and the linearity of $\mathbb{E}$, we rewrite it as 
\[
\mathbb{E}\left(
\underset{\epsilon'\downarrow 0}{\mathrm{l.i.m.}}~\e_{\epsilon'}-\e_\epsilon \right) 
\to 0 \quad \mbox{as} \quad \epsilon \downarrow 0.
\]
In other words, we must show that $\forall \delta>0$, $\exists~\bar{\epsilon}>0$ such that 
\begin{equation}\label{a:mustshow}
\left| \mathbb{E}\left( \e_{\epsilon'}-\e_{\epsilon}\right) \right|<\delta, \quad \forall \epsilon, \epsilon' < \bar{\epsilon}.
\end{equation}
Now consider the following string
\begin{align}
 \left|\mathbb{E}\left( \e_{\epsilon'}-\e_{\epsilon}\right)\right|  
&\leq  \mathbb{E}\left| \e_{\epsilon'}-\e_{\epsilon}\right|~~~ \mbox{(trivial)}\\
&\leq \left| \mathbb{E}\left( \e_{\epsilon'}-\e_{\epsilon}\right)^2 \right|^{1/2}\mbox{(Lyapunov)}
\end{align}
%The first inequality is trivial and the second one is Lyapunov's. 
But the convergence in the mean implies that $\forall \delta >0$, $\exists \bar{\epsilon}$
such that 
$\left| \mathbb{E}\left( \e_{\epsilon'}-\e_{\epsilon}\right)^2 \right|^{1/2}<\delta$, $\forall \epsilon, \epsilon' < \bar{\epsilon}$ 
Hence Eq.~\eqref{a:mustshow} holds and hence the result.
