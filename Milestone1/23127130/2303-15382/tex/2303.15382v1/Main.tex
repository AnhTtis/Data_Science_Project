\title[TTCs on $\der$ for varieties of general type]{Tensor triangulated category structures in the derived category of a variety with big (anti-)canonical bundle}
%\author{Angel Toledo}
\author{Angel Toledo}
\address{ Laboratoire J. A. Dieudonné UMR CNRS-UNS 7351.
Université Côte d'Azur, Nice, France}
\email{toledo@unice.fr}
\date{}
\maketitle
\begin{abstract}
    Let $X$ be a smooth projective variety over $\mathbb{C}$ with big (anti-)canonical bundle. It is known that in this situation the Balmer spectrum of the tensor triangulated category of perfect complexes $Perf(X)$ of $X$ equipped with the derived tensor product $\dtee$ recovers the space $X$. In this work we study the possible tensor triangulated category structures one can put on $Perf(X)$. As an application we prove a monoidal version of the well-known Bondal-Orlov reconstruction theorem.
\end{abstract}
