\section*{Highlights}%
% ====================

\begin{itemize}
    \item The paper presents three experimental studies on prompt engineering, the creative practice of writing and improving prompts for text-to-image generative models.
    The studies explore whether untrained participants can 1) recognize the quality of prompts, 2) write prompts, and 3) improve their prompts for generating digital artworks.
    \item Results indicate that special keywords in prompts are not intuitively applied by untrained participants.
    This suggests that prompt engineering is a % non-intuitive
    learned skill that requires expertise and practice.
    \item The paper provides ten recommendations for conducting experimental research on text-to-image generation and prompt engineering with participants recruited from paid crowdsourcing platforms.
    \item The paper speculates on four possible futures of prompt engineering.
\end{itemize}
