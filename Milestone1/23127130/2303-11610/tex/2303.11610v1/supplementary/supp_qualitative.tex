\section{Qualitative results} \label{sec:supplementary_qualitative}

In this section, we report additional qualitative results by comparing \ourmethod with EUMS$^\dag$~\cite{zhao2022novel} predictions.

Figs.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_poss0}-\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_poss3}, show qualitative results for SemanticPOSS from the split POSS-$4^0$ (Fig.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_poss0}), POSS-$3^1$ (Fig.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_poss1}), POSS-$3^2$ (Fig.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_poss2}) and POSS-$3^3$ (Fig.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_poss3}).

Figs.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_kitti0}-\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_kitti3}, show qualitative results for SemanticKITTI from the split KITTI-$5^0$ (Fig.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_kitti0}), KITTI-$5^1$ (Fig.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_kitti1}), KITTI-$5^2$ (Fig.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_kitti2}) and KITTI-$4^3$ (Fig.~\ref{fig:supp_qualitative_kitti3}). We add ground-truth labels as the supervised reference.
