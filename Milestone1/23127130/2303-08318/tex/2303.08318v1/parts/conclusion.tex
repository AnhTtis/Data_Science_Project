\section{Conclusion and Future Work}
In this work, we jointly incorporate social influence and tag relations into a user-video-tag network to enhance micro-video tagging.
To construct tag relations, we build a tag ontology based upon tag statistics and graphical constraints in a semi-supervised manner. 
To derive a better node representation in video-tag network, we present RADAR, a heterogeneous GNN composed of a gated graph transformer and adversarial aggregation network. Extensive experimental results on three datasets have demonstrated the superiority of RADAR, and the effectiveness of its two components.
