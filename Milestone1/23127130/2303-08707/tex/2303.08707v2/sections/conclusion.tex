\section{Conclusion}\label{sec_conc}
We provided explicit formulas for inputs that guarantee PE for linear and classes of nonlinear systems. For Hammerstein and locally reachable nonlinear systems (including SISO flat systems), we showed how to guarantee collective PE of input- and/or state-dependent basis functions. These results are crucial for the application of recent data-driven control schemes that require such PE conditions to be satisfied.
