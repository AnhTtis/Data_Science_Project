\section{List of optimizing parameters}
\label{app:list}

The present paper deals with the optimization of three groups of parameters containing 7, 13, and 21~numerical method parameters. These parameters correspond to the BiCGStab iterative method and the classical algebraic multigrid preconditioner. The Chebyshev polynomial method is chosen as both the pre- and post-smoother (with their own method parameters), and the direct solver is used at the coarsest level. The full list of the XAMG library parameters targeted for optimization and their equivalents in hypre are summarized in Table~\ref{tab:params}. The specific parameters included in optimization sets are shown in Table~\ref{tab:3sets}.
