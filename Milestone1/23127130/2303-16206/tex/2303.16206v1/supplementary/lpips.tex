\section{LPIPS Results}
\label{appendix:lpips}
In Table 3 of the main paper, we used PSNR and SSIM to evaluate image similarity between the cover images and the corresponding steganographic images. In \autoref{tab:lpips} we show the results of using (LPIPS)~\citep{zhang2018unreasonable}, a newer perceptual image similarity metric, to evaluate image similarity between cover and steganographic images. We observe similar trends as with PSNR and SSIM.

\hline
