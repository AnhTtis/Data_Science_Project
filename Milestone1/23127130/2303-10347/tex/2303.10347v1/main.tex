\begin{abstract}
We find all non-abelian generalizations of $\PI-\PVI$ \Painleve systems such that the corresponding autonomous system obtained by freezing the independent variable is integrable. All these systems have isomonodromic Lax representations. 
\medskip

\noindent{\small Keywords:  non-abelian ODEs, \Painleve equations, isomonodromic Lax pairs}
\end{abstract}
