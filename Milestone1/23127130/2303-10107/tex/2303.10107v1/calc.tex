\noindent \textit{ Introduction - }
\section{correlation function}
We are aiming at $WB_{1}$ conformal field theories. Since $A_1=B_1$, there will not be any $W$ operator with higher spins. We only have $L$ from Virosoro and $G$ from supersymmetry. Each primary field in $WB_1$ theory can be labelled by a vector $\Phi_{()}$. Here, our supercurrent operator is identified as $\Phi_{()}$.
