\section{Response to Reviewer 1's Comments}


\begin{enumerate}
\item { \color{blue}
It seems quite clear now that IDM did not fit the data well. My guess is that
there's not enough variability in the data for a good IDM fit. A major reason
is likely that the $a_{max}$ parameter is very low ($0.21 m/s^2$ in the previous fit, $0.05 m/s^2$ in the new one)
compared e.g. to $0.73 m/s^2$ proposed in the original IDM paper
and around $1.5 m/s^2$ fitted by Hoodendoorn \& Hoogendoorn.
The manuscript does not make major general claims of the comparative model performance, so I don't find this.
critical issue. That the poor fit of the IDM may be (partly) specific to this dataset should be mentioned
The abnormal parameters found should also be discussed briefly in section e.g. "C. Model Evaluation and Comparison".}
\end{enumerate}
