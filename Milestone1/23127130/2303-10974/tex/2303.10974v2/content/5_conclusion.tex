\section{Conclusion}

In this work, we propose a simple and effective approach to generate hallucinogens -- nonsensical gibberish  in one language that is translatable into another language by online translation tools. We evaluated our method on popular online translation systems -- Google, DeepL, and Yandex.  We found out that such systems process adversarial examples unpredictably: they not only translate nonsensical input in Russian but also can not translate seemingly meaningful English phrases.
